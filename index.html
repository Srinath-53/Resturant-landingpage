About

TAZ Restaurant in Hyderabad, specifically Taz’s Bhagyanagarm Biryani in Boduppal, is a well-known spot for affordable and flavorful meals, especially biryani. With a cozy indoor seating area and takeaway options, it offers a diverse menu that includes spicy biryanis, juicy kebabs, and Indo-Chinese favorites like Manchurian and fried rice. Loved by locals for its generous portions and quick service, it’s a go-to place for anyone craving a satisfying and budget-friendly meal in the area.

"Where every bite tells a story of Hyderabad’s spice and soul."
